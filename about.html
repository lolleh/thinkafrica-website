Leadership & Vision

Advancing equitable development

ThinkAfrica was founded on the principle that sustainable development in West Africa requires more than just external intervention; it demands local expertise, rigorous research, and a commitment to gender-transformative programming.

Under the guidance of our Executive Director, Zynab Binta Kamara, we have cultivated a culture of intellectual rigor and deep empathy. We believe that the most effective solutions are those co-created with the communities we serve, ensuring that our research and consulting services directly address the systemic challenges facing Sierra Leone and the broader West African region.

Our approach is defined by a posture of partnership: we listen first, analyze deeply, and act with precision. Whether we are developing sustainable livelihood programs or advising on good governance, our focus remains on building institutional capacity that empowers local actors to lead their own development journeys.

If you are representing a government agency, NGO, or private-sector institution looking for a credible partner in West Africa, we invite you to engage with our team. We are committed to transparency, excellence, and the pursuit of equitable development outcomes that create lasting change for youth, women, and marginalized communities across the region.
